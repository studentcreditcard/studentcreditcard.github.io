Axis Bank Credit Card for Jewellery Shopping: Discounts & EMI

Home / Blog / Blog Details

In today’s fast-paced world, financial flexibility is more important than ever. With rising inflation, fluctuating gold prices, and evolving consumer preferences, having a reliable credit card for jewellery shopping can make a significant difference. Axis Bank offers some of the best credit cards tailored for jewellery purchases, providing exclusive discounts, easy EMI options, and rewarding benefits. Whether you're planning a wedding, a festival purchase, or simply indulging in self-love, an Axis Bank credit card can enhance your shopping experience.

Why Choose an Axis Bank Credit Card for Jewellery Shopping?

Jewellery shopping is often a high-value transaction, and using the right credit card can unlock multiple advantages. Here’s why Axis Bank stands out:

1. Exclusive Discounts & Cashback Offers

Axis Bank frequently partners with leading jewellery brands like Tanishq, Kalyan Jewellers, Malabar Gold, and PC Jeweller to offer cardholders special discounts. These can range from 5% to 15% off on select purchases, making luxury more affordable.

2. Interest-Free EMI Options

Gold and diamond jewellery often come with hefty price tags. Axis Bank’s No-Cost EMI feature allows you to convert your purchase into easy monthly installments without additional interest charges, easing financial strain.

3. Reward Points & Accelerated Benefits

Every rupee spent on jewellery using an Axis Bank credit card earns reward points, which can be redeemed for future purchases, travel, or even statement credits. Premium cards like the Axis Bank Magnus or Axis Bank Select offer accelerated rewards on high-value spends.

4. Zero Foreign Markup Fees for International Purchases

If you're shopping for jewellery abroad, certain Axis Bank credit cards waive foreign transaction fees, ensuring you get the best exchange rates without hidden charges.

How to Maximize Savings with Axis Bank Credit Cards

1. Time Your Purchase During Festive Sales

Festivals like Diwali, Akshaya Tritiya, and Dhanteras often come with extra discounts (up to 20%) when using an Axis Bank credit card. Keep an eye on limited-time promotions.

2. Leverage EMI for Big-Ticket Purchases

Instead of paying upfront, split your payment into 3-12 months EMI with minimal processing fees. This helps manage cash flow without compromising on quality.

3. Combine Discounts with Reward Points

Stack brand discounts with reward points redemption for maximum savings. For example, use a 10% discount offer and then redeem points to further reduce the payable amount.

Global Trends Impacting Jewellery Shopping

1. Rising Gold Prices & Economic Uncertainty

With gold prices hitting record highs due to geopolitical tensions and inflation, consumers are turning to credit card EMI options to afford their purchases without liquidating savings.

2. Sustainable & Lab-Grown Diamonds

Millennials and Gen Z shoppers are increasingly opting for lab-grown diamonds and ethically sourced gold. Axis Bank’s tie-ups with eco-conscious jewellers provide exclusive deals on sustainable jewellery.

3. Digital Gold & NFT-Backed Jewellery

The rise of digital gold investments and blockchain-based jewellery ownership is changing how people shop. Some Axis Bank cards offer cashback on digital gold purchases via platforms like MMTC-PAMP.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Ignoring EMI Terms: Always check processing fees and tenure before opting for EMI.
  • Overlooking Fine Print: Some discounts apply only to specific jewellery categories (e.g., gold coins excluded).
  • Missing Reward Expiry Dates: Use accumulated points before they lapse.

Axis Bank credit cards redefine jewellery shopping by blending affordability, convenience, and luxury. Whether you're investing in heirlooms or gifting a special piece, the right card can make every purchase shine brighter.
